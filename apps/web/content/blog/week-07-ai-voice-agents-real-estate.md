## What AI Voice Agents Do for Real Estate Teams

### Immediate Lead Response (Inbound and Outbound)

When a prospect submits a contact form on Zillow, Realtor.com, your website, or any integrated source, QuickVoice can call them back within 30–90 seconds — while they're still looking at the listing.

The call is warm, specific, and human-sounding:

> "Hi, is this Sarah? Great, this is Jordan calling from Premier Homes — I saw you were interested in the property at 1847 Maple Drive. Do you have a moment to talk about it?"

The AI qualifies the lead during this call:
- Are they a buyer or seller?
- What's their timeline? (Just browsing vs. moving in 3 months)
- Have they been pre-approved for a mortgage?
- What neighborhoods and price ranges interest them?
- Do they have an agent already?

Qualified leads get transferred to an available agent or booked for a follow-up call or showing appointment — instantly, without the prospect ever knowing they spoke to AI first.

### After-Hours Inquiry Handling

**31% of real estate inquiries come in after 5 PM or on weekends.** These are the most motivated buyers — they're spending their evenings and weekends researching properties because they're serious.

Without AI:
- After-hours calls go to voicemail
- 40% of callers who reach voicemail don't leave a message
- Of those who do, 34% have booked with another agent by the time you call back

With QuickVoice:
- Every after-hours call gets answered in under 1 second
- The AI qualifies the prospect, answers property questions, and books showings
- The agent wakes up to a calendar full of qualified appointments

### Showing Scheduling and Confirmation

When a lead is ready to see a property, the AI handles the full scheduling flow:
1. Checks the agent's calendar for availability
2. Offers showing times based on mutual availability
3. Books the appointment and sends confirmation to both parties
4. Sends a reminder call/SMS 24 hours before
5. If the prospect needs to reschedule, handles it in the reminder call

The agent's calendar fills automatically. They wake up, check their schedule, and go to showings — no back-and-forth phone tag.

### Property FAQ Answering

When leads call with questions about specific listings, the AI can answer:
- Price, square footage, bedrooms/bathrooms
- Year built, recent renovations
- HOA details and fees
- School districts
- Days on market
- When the property is available for showing
- Neighborhood information

For more complex questions ("What's the seller's motivation?" "Are there any offers?"), the agent qualifies for escalation to the listing agent.

### Database and Sphere of Influence Follow-Up

Most agents have hundreds of contacts in their CRM who haven't been touched in months or years. AI outbound campaigns change this:
- Call everyone in the sphere of influence on a quarterly basis
- Ask about their real estate plans ("Just checking in — are you still planning to sell this spring?")
- Identify active buyers and sellers from dormant contacts
- Book discovery calls for warm leads

A 200-person sphere of influence, called quarterly by AI, generates 3–8 active listings or buyer referrals per quarter for a typical agent — without any manual dialing.

## Compliance Considerations for Real Estate AI Calling

### TCPA Compliance
The Telephone Consumer Protection Act governs automated calling. Key requirements:
- **Written consent required** before automated calls to cell phones for marketing purposes
- **Do Not Call registry compliance** — always scrub against DNC lists
- **Disclosure** — callers must be informed they're speaking with AI
- **Opt-out mechanism** — callers can say "stop calling me" and must be removed from future outreach

QuickVoice's real estate template includes: DNC scrubbing (integrated with national and state registries), automatic disclosure ("I'm Jordan, an AI assistant"), and instant opt-out processing.

### Fair Housing Act
Real estate agents must be careful that any qualifying questions don't violate Fair Housing law (cannot discriminate based on race, color, national origin, religion, sex, familial status, or disability). Configure your AI qualification scripts to ask only: timeline, price range, desired features, mortgage pre-approval status, and buyer/seller status.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can the AI show properties?**
No — AI voice agents are a pre-showing tool. They qualify leads, book showings, and answer factual questions. The showing itself requires a human agent.

**What if a prospect asks a question the AI can't answer?**
The agent handles gracefully: "That's a great question for [Agent Name] — let me book a 15-minute call for you, and they can answer that and walk you through the listing in more detail." Most objections to AI in real estate are satisfied by a clean handoff to a human.

**Does the AI know about all my active listings?**
You configure the AI with your listing inventory, which you can update as listings come and go. For MLS-integrated platforms, this can be automated.

**Can the AI handle Spanish-speaking callers?**
Yes. QuickVoice supports 100+ languages. Configuring a bilingual agent (answers in Spanish if the caller speaks Spanish, English if English) is a single configuration option that dramatically expands your accessible market.

# 7 Best Retell AI Alternatives in 2026

Retell AI has positioned itself as a hybrid voice agent platform -- part visual builder, part API -- that appeals to semi-technical teams who want some drag-and-drop convenience without fully giving up developer access. It is a reasonable middle-ground platform, but that middle ground comes with its own set of tradeoffs.

If you have been evaluating Retell AI and wondering whether another platform better fits your needs, this guide compares the 7 best alternatives available in 2026 with detailed analysis of features, pricing, compliance, and ideal use cases.

---

## Why Look for Retell AI Alternatives?

Retell AI has strengths, but teams commonly look for alternatives for these reasons:

- **Compliance certifications still in progress:** Retell AI lists its SOC 2 and HIPAA compliance as in progress rather than certified. For businesses in healthcare, financial services, or other regulated industries, "in progress" is not sufficient for deployment.
- **Neither fully no-code nor fully developer-friendly:** The hybrid approach means non-technical users still encounter technical barriers, while developers find the visual layer limiting compared to pure API platforms.
- **Limited native integrations:** Retell AI offers fewer pre-built connectors than platforms like QuickVoice. CRM and calendar integrations often require webhook configuration or custom development.
- **Smaller template library:** The pre-configured industry templates are fewer and less detailed than those offered by mature platforms, requiring more manual configuration.
- **Per-minute pricing at scale:** The pay-per-minute model starting at $0.07/min can become expensive for businesses processing high call volumes, with costs that are harder to predict month-to-month.
- **Language support gaps:** With support for 20+ languages, Retell AI falls short for businesses serving diverse multilingual customer bases.

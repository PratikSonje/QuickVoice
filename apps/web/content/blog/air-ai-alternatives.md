# 7 Best Air AI Alternatives in 2026

Air AI made waves in the AI voice agent market by promising fully autonomous sales agents that could handle entire phone conversations without human intervention. The platform targets sales organizations and positions itself as a replacement for human SDRs on outbound calling campaigns.

But Air AI's narrow focus, opaque pricing, annual contracts, and lack of compliance certifications have led many businesses to look for alternatives that offer more flexibility, transparency, and breadth of capability. Whether you need more than just outbound sales, want transparent pricing, or require compliance for regulated industries, this guide covers the 7 best Air AI alternatives in 2026.

---

## Why Look for Air AI Alternatives?

Air AI has a specific value proposition, but several common issues drive teams to evaluate alternatives:

- **Sales-only focus:** Air AI is built exclusively for outbound sales conversations. If you also need inbound customer support, appointment scheduling, order status updates, lead routing, or any non-sales use case, Air AI cannot help.
- **No public pricing:** There is no pricing page. You must go through a sales process to get a quote, which creates evaluation friction and makes comparison shopping difficult. Teams frequently report that pricing is higher than expected.
- **Annual contracts:** Air AI typically requires annual commitments, limiting flexibility for businesses that want to test before committing long-term or that have seasonal calling patterns.
- **No compliance certifications:** Air AI does not publish HIPAA, SOC 2, ISO 27001, or PCI DSS compliance. This rules it out entirely for healthcare, financial services, legal, and other regulated industries.
- **Limited language support:** With support for approximately 10+ languages, Air AI cannot serve businesses with diverse multilingual customer bases.
- **Managed service model:** Air AI operates more as a managed service than a self-serve platform. This means less control over agent configuration, slower iteration cycles, and dependency on Air AI's team for changes.
- **Limited transparency:** Without public documentation of capabilities, limitations, and pricing, it is difficult to evaluate Air AI against alternatives on an apples-to-apples basis.
